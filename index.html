Methodology

This heatmap visualises the relative intensity of food bank-related Google searches across UK regions. It uses Google Trends data as a near-real-time proxy for demand — faster than official statistics, though not a direct measure of food bank usage.

Scores range from 0 to 100, representing relative interest. A score of 100 means peak search interest relative to other regions; a score of 50 means half that level. These are not absolute search volumes.

Important: High search volume may reflect genuine need, but can also be driven by media coverage, awareness campaigns, or seasonal patterns. This tool shows search behaviour, not actual food bank usage. For official statistics, see The Trussell Trust.
